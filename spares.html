What we can supply

We can supply all products that one might need for pleasure crafts and commercial vessels. Our network and reach is wide enough to accommodate all sorts of requests and needs.

  • Engine Components

    • Diesel engine parts, such as pistons, cylinders, gaskets, and fuel system components.

    • Propellers and propeller shafts.

    • Bearings and seals for the engine and other machinery.

  • Navigation and Communication Equipment

    • Radar systems.

    • GPS and navigation instruments.

    • Radio communication equipment.

  • Safety Equipment

    • Life rafts and life jackets.

    • Fire suppression system components.

    • Emergency signaling devices.

  • Electrical and Electronics

    • Wiring, connectors, and electrical panels.

    • Control systems and circuit boards.

    • Bulbs, LEDs, and other lighting components.

  • Hull and Deck Components

    • Anchors and anchor chains.

    • Winches and windlass systems.

    • Hatches and watertight doors.

  • Pumps and Piping

    • Cargo and ballast pumps

    • Bilge pumps and related components.

    • Water and sewage system components.
